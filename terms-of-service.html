Prayers, Saints & Saviors ("PSS", "the Network", "the Service") is an experimental decentralized peer-to-peer humanitarian coordination platform that operates using distributed browser-based infrastructure.

1.1 Technical Implementation

The service utilizes:

  • FT-DFRP (Fractal Toroidal Density Field Routing Protocol) - Proprietary routing algorithms with O(‚àön log n) complexity
